// About Us //

PAKISTAN RUBBER & TYRE COMPANY (PRTC)

We, Pakistan Rubber & Tyre Company (PRTC) are the distributors of Bridgestone tyres in Pakistan since 1953. Our line up covers tyres for Passenger Car, Light Truck, 4×4 / SUV, Truck and Bus, Industrial, Off-The-Road and Air Craft categories. We have a nation wide spread out dealership network with more than 100 dealers and sub-dealers in approximately fifty cities in Pakistan. The core of long-term relations with our dealers is based on the trust and confidence, which we have in each other since inception and with the passage of time, it’s deepening further. Our corporate offices provide quality products and best possible services to cater the need of the corporate sectors of Pakistan with around 500 satisfied clients. We provide direct services to the armed forces, port users, automobile sectors, airports, cement factories, fleet owners, construction companies and other all-scale corporate sectors of Pakistan. Our preeminence is to provide customized solutions and excellent after sales service to its valued clients. Commercial vehicle manufacturers and assemblers in Pakistan such as HinoPak, Isuzu, Nissan, Volvo, Renault also rely on our products and after sales services. We have two branch offices in Lahore and Rawalpindi and run two company operated sales and service centres in Karachi and Lahore. These outlets are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment where our trained and skilled staff provides superior services to the customers.

Bridgestone Pakistan

At, Bridgestone, our goal is to become a truly global company and to achieve “Dan-Totsu” (the absolute and clear leader in Japanese) in our industries. The roots of our company date back to 1931, when Brigestone Tire Co., Ltd, was established in Kurume, Japan. Anticipating the future potential of tyres for passenger cars and the international development of the business, founder Shojiro Ishibashi used and English translation of his surname for the name of the company i.e. Bridgestone. The Bridgestone Group’s mission is based on the words of its founder: “Serving Society with Superior Quality.” We aspire to offer the best for our customers and to society, not only in terms of our products, services and technology, but in all of our corporate activities, our commitment to quality stems from a passion for improving the safety and lives of people everywhere. To fulfill this mission, the Bridgestone Group has used the concept of ‘Foundation’ to demonstrate the sustained commitment of employees to provide its customers with world-class products and services and to serve the communities where Bridgestone does business. Headquartered in Tokyo, Brigestone supplies its products to over 150 nations and territories around the world.
